Wheel Alignment

Wheel alignment is adjusting the angles of wheels to the car manufacturer specifications. Depending on the driving conditions, wheels deviate from their specific position in the chassis and therefore, wheel alignment is required.

Wheel Balancing

Tyre balance describes the distribution of mass within the entire wheel. During rotation, asymmetries of mass are inevitable due to hop or wobble and these can cause ride disturbances such as vertical and lateral vibrations.

Tire Fitting

Tyre fitting seems like a regular requirement for most vehicle owners but it requires a certain level of precision and expertise. We offer tyre fitting services that can help you in any type of emergency.

Tire Rotation

Tyre rotation is the practice of interchanging the wheels and tyres of an automobile to one another. This way, even tyre wear is expected to extend the useful life of a set of tyres. The reason for tyre rotation is to reduce the uneven wear on each set of tyre and wheels.

Car Tires

The most bought car tyres in the Indian market are the bias tyres that are high-performance economic tyres. Such tyres are installed in regular cars such as SUVs, hatchbacks, and sedans. These tyres tend to give an optimal performance at considerably lower prices.

Patch & Puncture

Tyre tubes are becoming a thing of the past and tubeless have taken their place. This has significantly changed the old puncture repair methods as well. There are DIY plug and seal methods and tyre cement available in which you can plug a soft polymer into the tyre that dissolves over time and completely fills the damaged area

Exterior Wash

Exterior Wash is an alternative to the regular everyday wash. Application of shampoo, which is not generally used in the regular everyday wash, softens the dirt, making it easy the clean without the formation of scratches of swirl marks.Vacuuming the interiors ensures cleaning of dust and particles even in the corners, which is difficult to clean.

Interior Cleaning

This is a deep cleaning procedure for the interiors of the car. All kinds of stains and marks will be removed during this procedure, giving an enriched and new feel to the interiors of the car. Starting with the roof of the car, The dashboard, door pads, seats hand brake gear area, and the floor will be cleaned using a cleaners and brushes ensuring a stain free interior.

Rubbing & Polishing

This service is recommended for new cars and if paint protection treatment has been done in the last 6 to 8 months. Along with the cleaning of hard water marks along the beadings and logos, the glazing procedure ensures removal of dullness and gives a good shine to the surface. The final waxing of the surface prevents the formation of minor scratches and hard water marks on the surface

Waxing

After a clean wash for the car, it is important to protect the surface from fine dust,which causes minor scratches, eventually forming swirl marks. Application of wax on the surface not only gives the car a shiny finish, but also acts as a protective layer, preventing contaminations on the surface.
